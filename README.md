GXCraft
=======

Minecraft Classic for the Wii  
Requires a Nunchuk (Not enough buttons otherwise)

Controls
--------

**Shared**

Button | Action
--- | ---
DPAD | Movement
Nunchuk Stick | Looking
A | Place Block
B | Break Block
C | Inventory
1 | Toggle Flying Mode


**Not Flying**

Button | Action
--- | ---
'+' | Inventory Right
'-' | Inventory Left
Z | Jump

**Flying**

Button | Action
--- | ---
'+' | Move up
'-' | Move down
